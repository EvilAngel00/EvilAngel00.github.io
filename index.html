Live Classrooms

A free, simple way for teachers to give lectures with active and real time feedback.

About Live Classrooms

As a teacher have you ever dreamed about having live and direct feedback about your lectures ? Have you ever been worried your students might be doing something else entirely ?

As a student have you ever been too afraid to ask a question or to ask a teacher to slow things down ?
Every one of these things is now possible through Live Classrooms !

Live Classrooms is a free service allowing people to give and attend lectures with a dynamic fashion. Learn more about it by taking a look at the features.









Features

- Upload your own PDF Slides using Amazon S3
- Full chat support
- Live feedback
- Consult your classroom history
